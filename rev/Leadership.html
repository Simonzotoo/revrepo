Reverend Hannah Aba Yawson – President

Reverend Hannah Aba Yawson is a dedicated leader with over 25 years of experience in social policy, development, and ministry. As the President of HYM, she leads the mission to uplift vulnerable individuals and communities.

An experienced development practitioner, dynamic speaker, and revivalist, she’s passionate about guiding people toward holistic well-being and spiritual growth.

She’s also known for her work in trauma care and advocacy for women’s empowerment during her time on the Discharge Board of Nsawam Female Prison in Ghana.

Mr. Gideon Hosu-Porbley – Director

Gideon Hosu-Porbley is a seasoned research and M&E practitioner with 20+ years of experience. He brings expertise in project planning, strategy, and thematic areas like migration, education, and governance.

He’s held fellowships at the University of Sussex, UK, and lectures at Ashesi University, Ghana.

Dr. Christiana Adu-Takyi – Director

Dr. Christiana Adu-Takyi is a Paediatric Cardiologist with a heart for ministry. Her passion is to see children and young adults grow in faith and submission to God.

She leads medical outreaches in schools and focuses on both medical and spiritual care for the young.

Mr. Alfred Koduah – Director

Alfred Koduah supports communities in education, healthcare, and faith. He has 12+ years of experience in ministry and HR and is known for compassionate leadership and purposeful service.

He merges professional and spiritual guidance with a deep love for people.

Reverend Isaac Kwafo Yawson – Board Chairman

Rev. Isaac Yawson has 25+ years in business administration, academia, and consultancy. He’s a church finance and administration consultant and a university lecturer.

His focus includes spiritual care, church structure, and organizational improvement.
