Our Ministries & Projects

HYM believes in living out God's call to serve others through compassion and tangible support

Transforming Lives Through Service

Through our various outreach programs, we strive to bring comfort, guidance, and support to those who are often forgotten or overlooked

Correctional Outreach

Prisons Ministry

Bringing hope and redemption to individuals in correctional facilities through spiritual guidance, prayer, and Bible distribution. We help inmates encounter Jesus and experience God's love and forgiveness.

Our mission is to inspire lasting change and renew purpose, affirming that no one is beyond God's grace.

Youth Support

Juvenile Aftercare Program

Providing guidance and mentorship to young individuals transitioning back into their communities after detention. We offer skills training, emotional support, and spiritual counseling.

Through mentorship and community connections, we help them build positive, productive futures.

Family Support

Children of Incarcerated Mothers

Providing emotional and spiritual support to children with incarcerated parents through mentorship, education, and community activities.

We offer stability and encouragement, reminding each child they are loved and capable of achieving their dreams despite adversity.

Healthcare Support

Hospital Ministry

Extending God's love to those facing health challenges through prayer, encouragement, and companionship for isolated patients.

We provide financial support for medical expenses, helping families focus on healing and recovery during difficult times.

Spiritual Renewal

Transformative Revival Services

Collaborating with churches to organize powerful revival services that sharpen spiritual awareness and deepen connection with God.

These gatherings bring healing, deliverance, and salvation while empowering believers to live purposeful, balanced lives.

Women's Ministry

Women's Empowerment Programs

Equipping women through spiritual growth, skills development, and community support initiatives.

Our programs help women discover their God-given potential and become change agents in their families and communities.
