Welcome to Hannah Yawson Ministry

HYM believes in living out God’s call to serve others. Through our various outreach programs, we strive to bring comfort, guidance, and tangible support to those who are often forgotten or overlooked. Here’s a closer look at how we work to make a difference

Our Ministry Calling

We are delighted to welcome you to Hannah Yawson Ministries (HYM), a Christian NGO, a place where compassion meets action. We are the hands and feet of Christ, bringing hope, healing, and renewal to all we encounter. Our mission is rooted in Jesus’ call to serve the vulnerable and marginalized with love, dignity, and hope. Through our ministries, we seek to bring God’s light and healing to those in need, walking alongside them on their journey toward transformation.

Founded by Rev. Hannah Yawson, our ministry has grown from humble beginnings to become a beacon of hope in our community, touching thousands of lives through our various outreach programs and spiritual initiatives.

- Rev. Hannah Yawson

About HYM

HYM is a non-profit Christian organization dedicated to showing God's love through spiritual and social services. Guided by the teachings of Matthew 25:40, we believe that each person is worthy of compassion, respect, and the fullness of life.

Our Vision

Our vision is to be a beacon of hope, restoring dignity and transforming lives through the love of Christ.

Our Mission

HYM exists to reflect God’s love and compassion by serving those who are vulnerable and marginalized. We are dedicated to empowering individuals and communities through spiritual guidance, practical support, and acts of service that uplift, restore dignity, and inspire transformation.
